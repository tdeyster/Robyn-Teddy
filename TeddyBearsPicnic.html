Where's the Teddy Bears Picnic?

Down in the woods today!


About 40 minutes outside of Durango, Vallecito Reservior is on the edge of the San Juan National forest. If you are coming by air, the nearest airport is in Durango (40 minutes). With additional options in Cortez (1.5 hr), Albuquerque NM (4 hr), and Denver (6.5 hr). We'd recommend arranging carpooling especially if coming into the further away airports. We've set up a "group carpool" site to organize ridesharing to the event.
